History

The Ministry of Health, founded by Empowerment Party in 1962, aims to help the citizens if
Trinidad and Tobago to be at the best health that can be at. we dreamt of helping their country
in any way we could. Realizing how poor the health care was in the country, we were determined
to do something about it. After years of hard work, we were able to create the Ministry of Health,
a place dedicated to ensuring that all the health facilities on Trinidad and Tobago has what
they need to provide proper health care to our citizens.

Goal

Our main goal is to do whatever we can to provide the best when it comes to the health care
options for our people. By supplying the any medication, tools, machinery that may be needed,
we aim to make sure that every health facility, big or small, has everything it needs to treat
people for whatever health conditions they may face.
